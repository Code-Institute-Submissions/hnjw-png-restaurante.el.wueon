El Wueon, the new restaurant in town!

We are a homely restaraunt that focuses on health and flavour. We use techniques and spices from Chile. We focus on baking bread, homemade pasta and fresh empanadas all week long. As well many specialties through out the week, including beef, fish and vegatarian. We chose to not use any dairy products, but instead products based on nuts.

We focus on giving you a experience will you not forget, with service with passion as all our waiters are specialised in discovering the flavours you need to try.

We are open everyday in the Gothenburg harbor, except for Sunday. We rest, bake and revive ourselves for Monday.
